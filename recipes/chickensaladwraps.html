Chicken Salad Wraps

Description

This recipe for curried egg sandwiches is quick, easy and delicious. In just 15 minutes, you can make 4 servings of this filling sandwich.

ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into quarters
  • ¼ cup chopped onions
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 4 tablespoons fresh salsa
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 (10 inch or 25.4cm) flour tortillas
  • 12 lettuce leaves

Steps

  1. In a small bowl combine the chicken, onion, mayonnaise, salsa, salt and pepper. Mix together.

  2. Line each tortilla with two lettuce leaves, then divide chicken salad mixture evenly among each tortilla and roll up, or 'wrap'.
