Chicken Salad Wraps

Description

This recipe for Chicken Salad Wraps is a delicious and easy meal that can be made in just 10 minutes. Perfect for a picnic or lunch on-the-go, this wrap has a salsa twist that adds a touch of flavor.

ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into quarters
  • ¼ cup chopped onions
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 4 tablespoons fresh salsa
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 (10 inch or 25.4cm) flour tortillas
  • 12 lettuce leaves

Steps

  1. In a small bowl combine the chicken, onion, mayonnaise, salsa, salt and pepper. Mix together.

  2. Line each tortilla with two lettuce leaves, then divide chicken salad mixture evenly among each tortilla and roll up, or 'wrap'.
