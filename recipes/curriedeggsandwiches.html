Curried Egg Salad

Description

This recipe for curried egg sandwiches is quick, easy and delicious. In just 15 minutes, you can make 4 servings of this filling sandwich.

ingredients

  • 4 hard-cooked eggs, peeled and chopped
  • Â½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 slices bread

Steps

  1. Mix together mayonnaise and curry powder in a bowl. Gently stir in eggs, then season to taste with salt and pepper.

  2. Evenly divide between 4 slices of bread, top with remaining 4 slices.
